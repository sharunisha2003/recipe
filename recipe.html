Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my faimly for generation and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apple,peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375*F(190*C)
  2. In a boul,mix the sugar and cinnamon
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
